Testimonials

Walton Cash

Being a differently person, it was always difficult for me find out open opportunities of employment. The AZ employment first initiative helped me to apply to a lot of software developer opportunities. After interviewing with a few potential employers, I was able to narrow down to one awesome employer, all thanks to the help provided by AZ Employment first. I recommend this initiative to everyone who is differently abled.
